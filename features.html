What OpenRAP Offers You

1

Local

The device is placed in a local environment (classroom or village square) with locally curated, pre-filled content. This provides a great deal of control to local authorities on the type of content available to and viewed by students and teachers. It also allows content to be regularly updated.

 
 
2

Offline

All content is available on the local device. This allows users to easily access and download content without dependence on Internet connectivity.

 
 
3

Monitoring & Maintenance

The OpenRAP solution has in-built monitoring capabilties. It offers administrators dashboards to monitor usage and performance. This allows for easy and in-time device maintenance.

It is also extremely easy to upload content on the device. This allows content to be regularly updated.

Getting end-user usage reports is also fairly simple as user data is captured annonymously but automatically.
